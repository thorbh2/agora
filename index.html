AI-moderated treasury

Funding decided by judgment, not by whales.

Agora pools GEN under a written charter. Anyone proposes. Instead of a token vote, the contract reads each proposal against the charter and a validator set agrees on what deserves funding. Merit over balance.

A common treasury, governed by a shared mandate
The assembly

Every grant, weighed against one charter.

No lobbying, no token-weighted votes. Each proposal is read against the same written mandate, and the validator set scores its alignment. The treasury follows merit, not the size of a wallet.

  • One charter, applied to all
  • Validators score alignment 0-100
  • Approved grants pay from treasury

How a grant is decided

Propose

Submit a title, plan, and the GEN you request from the treasury. Make your case against the charter.

Review

The contract reads it against the charter; validators score alignment independently and must agree on the outcome.

Disburse

If approved and the treasury can cover it, the grant pays out on-chain. No vote, no veto, just alignment.
